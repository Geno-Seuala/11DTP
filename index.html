About the team

Northhaven high school, founded in 1947 is a high school contructed on the hillscapes of Auckland, and with it was founded a basketball team that is now known as an international beacon of athleticism, the team boasts a rich history of sucess and national domination. The boys work tirelessly, giving up hundreds of hours out of their year to be a part of our amazing program we have here at Northhaven. Statistics show our boys our over 200% more likely to pursue a professional basketball career than any other school in the world! The boys have won numerous NCAA championships and have had victories all across the globe. It is for these reasons that we hold such an incredible reputation with not only our players, parents, and managers but our sponsors as well. Our program is proudly sponsored by large cooporate basketball providers with name like: Dr.Dish, Wilson, Nike, & Gatorade.

What is Basketball?

Basketball. A game originating from an American YMCA in 1891, is a game played by up to 26 players on 10 foot tall iron hoops and tempered glass backboards in which both teams must advance a leather ball up their respective sides of the court in the hopes of scoring a "basket". The game has evolved tremendously over the last 100+ years. With new rule changes, associations, and sponsorships, the basketball industry is now worth well over ten billion dollars and is proving to be a viable career path for many young athletes. At Northhaven, we provide our athletes with exceptional athletic training, incredible recovery and meal plans, and top-tier education opening doors to virtually every industry outside of basketball. This is made possible by our incredible team of staff, lead my Mr. Steven Pingdale, our program stands tall amongst a nation of outstanding athleticism as a beacon of premier development and learning.
